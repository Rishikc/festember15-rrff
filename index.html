Rolling Reels International Film Festival

An initiation on behalf of Festember, Rolling Reels Intenational Film Festival aims to cultivate and inspire people to capture their ideas on celluloid. The film festival shall be held in Hyderabad on August 15th and 16th. Short films of all genres and languages are accepted as entries.

With the crème of the crop and the amateurs participating, the competition is sure to be invigorating, entertaining and unique. Apart from the screening of the shortlisted films there are going to be industry specific workshops conducted by With eminent personalities and prominent celebrities, Rolling Reels hopes to enthral all those who participate.

Prizes

Prizes are awarded for the following categories:

  • Best film
  • Second best film
  • Best director
  • Best screenplay
  • Best cinematography
  • Best editing
  • Best sound mixing
The highlight is the “Ulaganayagan Kamal Haasan Award” which is to be a rolling trophy awarded to the best actor.

The Venue

Situated in the heart of Telangana, Hyderabad is a city which has been blessed with a rich film culture. It is the home to Ramoji Film City, the largest film studio in the world and has also given birth to some of the biggest stars in the film industry. The Finale is set to take place at Annapurna International School of Film and Media, India’s first private non-profit media school which is fast evolving as one of the India’s largest.

Rules

Rules of the event (quick overview here)

All that we ask you is that you give original work. It need not be brilliant but hard work is appreciated all the same. Please stick to the deadline of 15th July, 2015. Make sure that your film does not exceed the running time of 20 minutes. Multiple entries are allowed so all the more chances for you. We wish you all the best.
